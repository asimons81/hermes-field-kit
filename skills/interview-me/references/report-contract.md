# Report Contract

## Allowed classifications

- `READY TO PROCEED`
- `PROCEED WITH ASSUMPTIONS`
- `PAUSED`
- `STOPPED`

## Required headings

- Interview Outcome
- Objective
- Confirmed Context
- Constraints
- Preferences
- Tradeoffs and Decisions
- Unknowns
- Recommended Next Step

Do not invent an intermediate classification. Put nuance in confidence, warnings, blockers, and Not Verified.
